About

I came to the USA four years ago. Before that, I was a pretty good electrician in Poland (there is magic happening when you make all those bulbs glow), but from the very beginning, when I moved to Chicago, I knew that one day I would become a web developer. That was my plan and also a dream – and it is hard to find a better place to make your dreams come true than America, right?

That is why I decided to join Thinkful as a student, which I currently am, and start developing my coding skills. And I already know that it was a great decision and I could not be happier! Creating web pages that impress, improving myself by taking inspiration from well-designed projects, collaborating with creative minds who inspire and push me further and further to cross the boundaries of my skills, that is what makes me thrilled.

Although I enjoy the front end side of the web development the most, I also find pleasure in solving problems by writing functions. Breaking down a problem into smaller problem-solving tasks, writing code that bravely gets even with any encountered issue - this is gratifying. Don't you agree?

In my free time, I let my artistic side possess me and create some art. I am focused on photography and graphic designs. You can see some of my works in my portfolio on Behance.

Whenever I look for an inspiration I find it in one of these places:

  • designchapel
  • Behance.net
  • youtube.com

Things that boost my creativity (not necessarily in that order):

  1. a sip of a coffee
  2. listening to music
  3. strolling along
  4. adding a pinch of chaos to the daily boredom
